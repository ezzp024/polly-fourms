Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and any profile information you provide (display name).

Content: We store threads, replies, and other content you post.

Usage Data: We may collect basic usage analytics such as page views and interaction patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our forum services
  • To notify you about replies to your threads
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To respond to your requests and support needs

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase (a cloud database service). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for database services and GitHub Pages for hosting. Each has their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us through the forum or use the account deletion feature in your profile settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy here.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please use the forum's report system or contact the administrator.
