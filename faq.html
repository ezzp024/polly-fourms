Frequently Asked Questions

Search or browse common questions below.

How do I create an account?

Click "Login" in the navigation, then use the Register form. Enter your email and a password (10+ characters). Check your email for a verification link.

How do I log in?

Click "Login" in the navigation, then use the Login form with your email and password.

How do I set my display name?

After logging in, go to your Profile page. Enter your desired display name and click Save. This name will appear on your posts.

How do I create a new thread?

Navigate to a forum section (like Software Releases or Coding Help), then use the "Post New Thread" form at the top.

How do I reply to a thread?

Open any thread and scroll to the "Post Reply" form at the bottom. Enter your message and click Submit.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the "Report" button on any thread or reply. Select a reason for the report. Our moderators will review it.

Where can I find software downloads?

Click "Downloads" in the navigation or visit the Software Releases section.

What do Pin, Sticky, Lock, and Hide mean?

Pin: Keeps thread at top. Sticky: Keeps thread near top. Lock: Prevents new replies. Hide: Removes thread from public view. These are moderator tools.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Use the "Resend Verification Email" form on the login page. For full password reset, contact an administrator through the report system.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile page and look for account settings. Contact an administrator if you need assistance.

Can I format my posts?

Yes! Use markdown: **bold**, *italic*, `code`, ## headers, and more.

Is there a dark mode?

Click the moon/sun icon in the navigation bar to toggle between light and dark themes.

Can I bookmark threads?

Click the "Bookmark" button on any thread to save it for later. Your bookmarks are stored in your browser.

Are there keyboard shortcuts?

Yes! Press / to search, ? for help, Alt+N for new thread, Alt+H for home, Alt+C to compose.
